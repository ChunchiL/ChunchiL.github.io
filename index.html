About

Dr. Chunchi Liu is a postdoctoral researcher at the Institute of Natural Sciences, Shanghai Jiao Tong University. She received her Ph.D. from East China Normal University in 2025.

Her research lies in numerical analysis and scientific computing, with particular interests in numerical methods for partial differential equations, stochastic and parametric problems and dynamical low-rank approximation.

Her current research focuses on randomized low-rank methods for structure-preserving simulations of open quantum systems, especially the Lindblad master equation.
